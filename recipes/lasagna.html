Lasagna

  • Sugar
  • SPice and seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before service
