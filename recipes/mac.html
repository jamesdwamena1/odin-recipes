Mac and Cheese


I would love to try Mac And Cheese

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Teaspoon salt
  • Black pepper
  • Milk
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil Macaroni
  2. Melt Butter
  3. Add flour, salt and pepper and stir
  4. Pour milk while stirring
  5. Add cheddar cheese
  6. Drain and add macaroni
  7. Ready to serve
